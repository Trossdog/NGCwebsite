SMS Terms of Service

By opting into SMS from a web form or by providing your mobile number, you agree to receive SMS messages from Neighborhood General Contractor. These messages may include updates on your project, appointment reminders, and general service notifications.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary based on project status and customer communication needs.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opting Out

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Help

For help or support, reply HELP to any message or contact us at info@neighborhoodgc.com.

Contact Us

For more information, visit our website or contact us directly.
